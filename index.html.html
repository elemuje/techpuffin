Redefining Tech Education in Africa

TechPuffin Academy was founded with a clear mission: to democratize access to cutting-edge tech skills across Africa. We believe that regardless of your background or prior experience, you deserve the tools to build a profitable tech career.

We're not just another coding bootcamp. We train the next generation of Vibe Coders — developers who combine traditional programming knowledge with the power of AI to build faster, smarter, and earn more.

⚡ What is Vibe Coding?

Vibe Coding is a modern, AI-augmented approach to software development. Instead of memorizing every line of syntax, Vibe Coders use AI tools like Claude, GPT, and GitHub Copilot to accelerate building — while understanding the fundamentals needed to direct, refine, and deploy real products.

Absolutely not! TechPuffin Academy is designed to be beginner-friendly. If you can use a smartphone or basic computer, you're ready. We start from the very beginning and build you up week by week.

You can follow along with a laptop, desktop, or even an Android smartphone using Termux (covered in Week 2). A laptop is recommended for the best experience, but we've designed the curriculum to be accessible on mobile too.
